Documents are dumped in a simple XML format.

Example:

<documents>
	<document>
		<meta name="title">The title</meta> <!-- not implemented till now -->
		<meta name="encoding">utf-8</meta> <!-- not implemented till now -->
		<meta name="downloaded-from">http://example.org</meta>
		<meta name="downloaded-on">11-01-2008 15:00:00Z</meta>
		<content>
			Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequat.
		</content>
  <document>
</documents>
